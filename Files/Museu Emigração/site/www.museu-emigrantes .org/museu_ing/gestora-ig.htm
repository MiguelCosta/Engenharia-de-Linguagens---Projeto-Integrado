In the installation phase, the City council of Fafe will approve the structure, organization and functioning of the museum space, nominate the Director/Coordinator of the project and elaborate protocols with the involved institutions and people.

 

 

Project Director / Coordinator


 

The nomination of the project Director  is one of the functions of the managing and instituting entity of the project. They have to plan the installation of the museum space, as well as promote all the activities of the  project, defining the structure, organization and functioning of the museum space.

 

Plan the activities to be developed.

 

To promote contacts with the involved collaborators and institutions in the project. To promote the creation, installation and functioning of the Documentary and Computing Centres.

 

To promote contacts with different entities, institutions and people who can become relevant to the development of the project, on behalf of the instituting entities.

 

Propose the creation of protocols, defining their characteristics and objectives.

Museum Specialist


 

 Nominated by the instituting entities, he will define the strategic directions and activities of the museum space.

 

Document Resources Director

 

 

The document specialist will collect, choose and organize the bibliography and specific documentation on national, regional and local emigration, as well as of other sources and registers of the phenomenon of the mobility and return in its multiple expressions.

 

The Centre of Documentation will have to proceed to the exhausting survey of national, district and municipal identifying registers of emigrants in Portugal, as well as abroad, through the consulates.

 

To identify, collect and organize scientific bibliography referring to the migratory phenomenon, in the scientific and literary domains.

To collect private archives of families of emigrants and other forms of reconstitution of Histories of life mainly through verbal History.

 

To organize registers of iconographic information: old postcards, photograph and other sources of significant visual information for the study of the emigration and return.

 

To proceed with the survey concerning the expressions of return emigration in all the dimensions of transformation and characterization of the nineteenth and twentieth centuries.

 

 

 

 

Organization

 

Searchers / Scholars

 

 

 The searchers, natives or living in Brazil and other South-American countries, Portugal, Europe, North America, Africa and Asia, constitute the consulting board of the Museum and will find a place to divulge their studies in the ROOM Of KNOWLEDGE.

 

 

Entities with a collaborating protocol

 

 

The Portuguese and foreign cities, as well as  cultural institutions will be able to adhere to the project, creating delegations of the museum space, contributing for the functioning and general widening of the system centrally managed. The Camões institute; Portuguese consulates abroad; Town Councils and/or twinning protocol; the National Association of Town Councils.

 

 

Municipal Delegations

 

 

The cities will be able to become partners through the creation of adhesion protocols, studied in detail, fulfilling the directions of the project, in the activities that can feed the Database, as well as in activities benefiting from the divulging of its history.

 

 

Collaborators  and visitors

 

 

This project is conceived for the active participation of the visiting emigrants, Portuguese descendants or Associations of emigrants, using the ROOM of  the DIASPORA, for the nominal identification and stories describing the emigrant experience, which will feed the Database. This Room will also grow in importance with the permanent collaboration of academic institutions and the participation of the consulates.

 

 

Installation

 

It will have easily identifiable architectural characteristics with the migrant phenomenon (possibly the house of a Brazilian or a significant property), in which it is possible to detect contexts of the migrant experience, expressed in documents and pieces of furniture and iconography of that  time; a place where the archive of the Centre of Documentation can be set up and cultural activities of thematic spreading can be developed.

 

 

Project Financing

 

A wide project in intentions and structured for a long period has the support of the City Council of Fafe, providing installations, equipment, technical and human resources.

 

The project can expect the financial support of the Secretary of State for Emigration and the Secretary of State for the Communities, the budgetary endowments proceeding from associated cities, the money-making services and the Association of Friends of the Museum, either private or companies.

 

The project may get the contribution of the Portuguese Institute of Museums and the national and foreign related institutions
